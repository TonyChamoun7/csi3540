About Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, founded in 1902, is one of the most successful and recognized football clubs in the world. Known for their iconic white kits, they have won numerous domestic and international trophies, including a record number of UEFA Champions League titles.

Notable Achievements

  • Multiple La Liga titles
  • Numerous UEFA Champions League trophies
  • FIFA Club of the 20th Century
  • Home to legendary players like Di Stéfano, Zidane, Cristiano Ronaldo, and more
